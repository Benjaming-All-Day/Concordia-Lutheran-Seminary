Concordia Lutheran Seminary (CLS) is a seminary of Lutheran Church-Canada located in Edmonton, Alberta. CLS a member of the International Lutheran Council in partnership with Concordia Lutheran Theological Seminary, St. Catharines. CLS is accredited by the Commission on Accrediting of the Association of Theological Schools in the United States and Canada.
